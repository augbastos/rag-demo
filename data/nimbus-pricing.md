# Nimbus — Plans & Pricing

## Free
For personal use. Unlimited notes, local-first sync across two devices, Markdown
and PDF export, 30 days of version history. No cost, no card required.

## Pro — €6 / month
Everything in Free plus unlimited devices, unlimited version history, offline
full-text search and priority support. Billed monthly or €60/year.

## Team — €10 / user / month
Everything in Pro plus shared notebooks, real-time collaboration, comments,
per-notebook permissions and end-to-end encryption. Minimum two users.

## Business — custom
Everything in Team plus SSO/SAML, audit logs, a dedicated data region and a
customer success contact. Contact sales for a quote.

Annual billing saves roughly two months on every paid plan. You can switch or
cancel any time; downgrades keep all existing notes accessible on Free.
