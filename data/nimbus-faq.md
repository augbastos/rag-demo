# Nimbus — Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I use Nimbus offline?
Yes. Nimbus is local-first: every note is stored on your device, so you can read
and edit completely offline. Changes sync automatically the next time you
reconnect, with conflicts resolved per-paragraph rather than per-file.

## How do I export my notes?
Open Settings → Export. You can export a single note or your whole workspace as
Markdown, PDF, or a portable `.nimbus` archive. Markdown export preserves tags,
links and attachments in a plain folder structure you fully own.

## Is my data encrypted?
Notes are encrypted at rest on device and in transit. On the Team and Business
plans you can turn on end-to-end encryption, where Nimbus servers only ever see
ciphertext and cannot read your content.

## Can I collaborate with other people?
Team and Business workspaces support real-time collaboration: shared notebooks,
live cursors, comments and per-notebook permissions. Free and Pro are single-user.

## What happens if I cancel?
Your notes are never locked. After cancelling you drop to the Free plan and keep
full read/write access to everything already created; only paid features (E2E
encryption, collaboration, unlimited history) switch off.
